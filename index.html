Thank you for checking out my tutorial of Dota 2. This game means a whole awful lot to me. However, I know it can be scary to get into because of its complexity and/or notorious community.

Allow this guide to be a useful companion on your journey to learning one of the most rewarding competitive multiplayer games ever made.

Good luck and have fun!
